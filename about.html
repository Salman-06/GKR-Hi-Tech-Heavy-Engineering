About Us

Where Vision Meets Foundation

Founded in 2023, GKR HITECH HEAVY ENGINEERING is headquartered in Coimbatore and backed by over a century of combined expertise from our managing partners. Specializing in precision machining and fabrication, we deliver innovative industrial solutions.

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company by TUV INDIA, our facility is equipped with advanced production technologies and supported by a highly skilled engineering team.
